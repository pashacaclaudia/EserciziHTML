Tokyo

Tokyo, officially Tokyo Metropolis (東京都 Tōkyō-to), one of the 47 prefectures of Japan,
has served as the Japanese capital since 1869.
As of 2014, the Greater Tokyo Area ranked as the most populous metropolitan area in the world.

Tokyo is often referred to as a city but is officially known and governed as a "metropolitan prefecture",
which differs from and combines elements of a city and a prefecture, a characteristic unique to Tokyo.
